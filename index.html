Environmental protection

Environmental incidents include natural and non-natural hazards. Such accidents always bring significant damage to nature and people. Fast and accurate response will contribute to avoid heavy consequences that can last for decades. By taking care of the nature and environment, we first of all create a future for the next generations.  Arete - designs and manufactures equipment for oil spill response, hazardous substance accidents, decontamination systems and flood protection solutions

Training facilities for first responders

Training exercises are one of the most important actions of everyday duty for firefighters and rescuers. We are offering wide range of mobile and permanently installed solutions. Our innovative control station outperforms your expectations. Arete Logic software controls the training processes on every step and give the ability to analyze training scenarios as well as individual skills of every trainee.

Storage and transport solution

The various types of ISO Containers and customized superstructures Arete Technologies manufacturing for the transportation, storage and deployment of any rescue, military and emergency equipment. The certified hook-lift platforms and forklift pockets allows the superstructures to be moved by truck, vessel or helicopter. The containers can be equipped with several door access options, re-tractable roof, hydraulically operated floors, air conditioning, heating and a wide variety of different furnish options. In addition to transportation of the equipment the container is especially designed for storage and deployment of any equipment and customised outside colours and signage are optional.
